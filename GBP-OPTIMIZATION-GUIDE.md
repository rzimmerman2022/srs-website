## Advanced Tips & Troubleshooting

### If You're Not Seeing Results After 60 Days

**Low Search Volume?**
- ‚úÖ Double-check service area is set correctly
- ‚úÖ Verify primary category is "Resume Service" or "Career Counselor"
- ‚úÖ Add more secondary categories
- ‚úÖ Use more location keywords in business description

**Low Actions (Calls/Clicks)?**
- ‚úÖ Make phone number more prominent in description
- ‚úÖ Add stronger CTAs in posts
- ‚úÖ Improve photo quality (photos drive clicks)
- ‚úÖ Add more services (more ways to engage)

**Not Ranking in "Local Pack" (top 3)?**
- ‚úÖ Get more reviews (velocity matters)
- ‚úÖ Add more photos (activity signal)
- ‚úÖ Post more frequently (3x per week minimum)
- ‚úÖ Ensure NAP consistency across ALL web properties
- ‚úÖ Build citations (Yelp, Bing Places, Apple Maps)

### Pro Tips from 7 Years of GBP Management

**Tip 1: Respond to Reviews FAST**
- The faster you respond (under 24 hours), the more Google favors your profile
- Set up Google Business Profile app on phone for instant notifications

**Tip 2: Use Google Posts as SEO Content**
- Google indexes post content - use keywords naturally
- Link posts to specific service pages on your website

**Tip 3: Refresh Old Photos**
- Delete and re-upload photos every 6 months (signals freshness)
- Always use different file names when re-uploading

**Tip 4: Leverage "Booking" Button**
- If you use Calendly or similar, add booking link to GBP
- Lowers friction for consultation scheduling

**Tip 5: Monitor "Search Queries" Report**
- Found in Insights - shows EXACT phrases people use
- Use these phrases in your business description and posts

**Tip 6: Answer Questions Publicly**
- Even if someone emails you a question, add it to Q&A section
- Helps future prospects (and gives Google more content to index)
